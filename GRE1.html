The 1982 Walking Horse Association Awards Party

\n

\n Here is a treasured photo from the 1982 Walking Horse Association awards party held at the beautiful Grand Ole Opry House. It showcases the joy and camaraderie of the participants as they celebrated their achievements.\n

\n \n

\n One of the highlights of the evening was the performance by the Ghost Riders Express. Their electrifying song filled the room with energy and passion.\n
